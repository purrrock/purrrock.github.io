Windowsill reflections

Альбом "Hero Cats" (2025)

I still recall those fearless nights, The rooftops high, the boldest fights. The queens would watch me from afar, Then I’d slip off, a fading star. Nibble, Nibble, brave and daring! Stories, tales, are amazing. World so vast, with lights a-shining, Courage strong and paws a-flying. Together on the windowsill, We watch the world, both calm and still. One dreaming back, while one looks wide, Yet both find peace, where we reside. Oh, Pips, the world is big and wild, But here you're safe, my daring child. Let’s warm ourselves by oven heat, And wait for food in bowl to eat. But Nibble, can we hide away? The world is wide, it calls to play. I want to sail the oceans free, And soar like birds above the sea. Yes, Pips, the world is beautiful, I say, But peace and comfort here to stay. The fridge door creaks, it calls my name, To eat, to rest, life soft and tame. Together on the windowsill, We watch the world, both calm and still. One dreaming back, while one looks wide, Yet both find peace, where we reside. The day drifts down, as dusk draws near, We watch as shadows disappear. Two souls, abreast, as night descends, Our purrs a song that never ends.
